About

Hello, I’m ryuseiyellow! I’m somewhat of a novice translator hoping to improve my Japanese with song translations. I studied Japanese in high school for 5 years, and hope to keep my skills sharp via translation!

Whilst I mostly focus on Vocaloid music, I might occasionally translate non-Vocaloid too: Enstars music, MILGRAM music, and Shinsei Kamattechan are what I find myself listening to often, although Vocaloid certainly takes up majority of my playlist.

I upload my Vocaloid translations to the Vocaloid Lyrics Wiki if there’s not already an accepted translation for the song.

I also have a YouTube channel, where I indulge my hobbies besides translation. It’s mostly Enstars stuff there (yikes…)

Besides translating, I love personality typology, drawing, singing (poorly), and coding. I’m currently studying web development in college! Neat.

Thank you for visiting! I hope you enjoy my translations.
